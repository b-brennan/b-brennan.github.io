About Me

Hello! My name is Ben Brennan, I'm a Lead Application Developer at UniFirst Corporation where I spearhead the development of enterprise-grade applications and I'm a graduate of University of Massachusetts Amherst. At an early age I found a love for art, and for a while I was confident that I would pursue it as a career. After discovering programming and development in my later years of high school, I was searching for a way to combine my two passions.

This led me to discover the world of Web and Application Development, which perfectly blends my creative/artistic mindset with my problem-solving abilities. In my role at UniFirst, applications that I work on serve over 4,000 employees, focusing on creating scalable, efficient solutions that automate operations and enhance security. I'm passionate about building applications that make a real impact, whether it's automating manual processes, improving security, or enhancing user experience.

About Me

Hello! My Name is Ben Brennan, I'm currently studying Computer Science at the University of Massachusetts Amherst. My love for art came to me at an early age and for a while I was confident that I would persue that as a career. After discovering programming and development in high school, I knew there had to be some way to combine my two passions.

It wasn't until later on in my college career that I discovered the world of Web Development and Design. I knew right away that this is what I wanted to do as a career. It was a perfect blend of my creative/artistic and problem solving skill sets. I'm now looking for a full time job in Web Devlopment/Design. I have experience and familiarty in a variety of front-end developmental tools, and a working knowledge with back-end languages as well.
